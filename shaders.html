The Process

I'm not an artist — I'm a programmer. But I wanted to learn enough about shaders and Unity's URP to make my game characters look polished and stylized. Here's the journey, step by step.

01

Starting point: a free .fbx model from the Unity Asset Store. The colors are dull and the model doesn't stand out at all in the game world.

02

First pass with Unity's URP. The blurriness is gone and the colors are noticeably improved. Happy to see results right away.

03

Added a small outline on the character model and adjusted her skin tone to push toward an anime style. Not satisfied with most of it — but the eye textures came out great.

04

Applied a proper shader. The character now stands out even in a colorful world and has a clean anime-style outline. A massive improvement over step 1.

This is where my shader journey stops for now — the goal was always to apply this to my Unity MMORPG project to make it pop, and it does exactly that.
